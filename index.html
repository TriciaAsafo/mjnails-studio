About

At MJ Nails Studio, we blend art, care, and relaxation to give your hands and feet the pampering they deserve. Whether at our studio or in the comfort of your home, every detail is crafted for luxury.

Founded by Joyce Koffi-Simmons, MJ Nails is built on a passion for precision, hygiene, and exceptional client service. Whether you're visiting for a quick refresh or indulging in a full self-care session, our treatments are thoughtfully designed to promote both beauty and relaxation.
